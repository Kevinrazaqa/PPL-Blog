People Person

by Kevin Razaqa Aulia

Updated on May 2023

On this section i will focus on what i did as a team member of a SCRUM team to help our team more motivated and productive

Basically in order to keep our team on the highest performance me and my team did a couple of things.

First, we cheer each other up during the sprint. We did this for example on a daily meet to keep our team bond and try to avoid that "stessful" work environment.

Second, We also always try to help each other. For example if one of the team member had a problem with some task the other team member that maybe understand it more are willing to help in order to not only achieve our sprint goal but also just be a helpful person.

Third, motivate. This kind of the same like the first point. We always motivate each other during the sprint so that nobody feels their burden is too heavy or anything like that

Fourth, bring joy. We kind of always joke arounf after the daily meet so that we can also feel the bond of the team and be happy

Okay, this is one of an example of being a "people person" that keeps the spirit of the team. We can also give like maybe a chocolate or any other food for example my team also done this once to give the other apreciation ant keeping the bond among each others.
